DroneLogbook Solutions

DroneLogbook provides customized solutions for a wide range of industries, helping organizations manage drone operations more efficiently. Because education, public safety, energy, construction, and commercial sectors all operate differently, our platform is designed to be scalable, modular, and configurable—ensuring each industry receives the tools it needs.

For large enterprises and complex UAV programs, we offer a Private Label (White Label) version of DroneLogbook. This enhanced platform includes a robust administrative panel, configurable workflows, and custom notification systems to support advanced operational requirements

DroneLogbook For Commercial
DroneLogbook empowers commercial drone operators to efficiently plan, organize, and monitor their flight activities while maintaining compliance with industry regulations. The platform offers comprehensive tools for mission planning, fleet management, documentation, and reporting. As commercial UAV use grows worldwide, we remain committed to adding new capabilities and partnering with industry leaders to provide a streamlined end-to-end operations and compliance ecosystem.
DroneLogbook for Public Safety
www.safetydrone.org
Designed for emergency services, DroneLogbook’s Public Safety Edition provides tools and terminology tailored to the needs of police, fire, EMS, and emergency planning teams. The platform’s pricing model reflects the operational tempo and budget realities of public safety organizations.
For security and authenticity, only first responders registering with an official work email are granted access.
DroneLogbook for Education
Created for high-turnover training environments, DroneLogbook’s education solution makes it easy to enroll students, deliver training, track their flights, and rotate them off the platform—all while staying within a fixed budget.
It’s the perfect choice for drone, RPAS, and UAV training programs of all types, from four-year colleges to specialized workforce-retraining and upskilling courses.
DroneLogbook for Delivery
(Supporting delivery and BVLOS operations)
DroneLogbook is increasingly partnering with organizations advancing drone delivery and BVLOS operations, providing tailored solutions to support their evolving needs. We deliver customized operational workflows, maintenance and inspection tools, and reporting features built specifically for complex delivery ecosystems.
Our development team works directly with your operations group to create scalable, configurable solutions that match your unique mission requirements.
DroneLogbook for Agriculture
www.dronelogbook.ag
The DLB Ag Edition supports agricultural drone operators globally, offering a workflow built for spray missions, crop treatments, and precision-ag applications. By integrating advanced DroneLogbook features with agriculture-specific processes, it ensures a reliable and accurate flow of application data from growers and agronomists directly to the Remote Pilot executing the mission.
DroneLogbook for Manufacturers
Drone manufacturers can leverage DroneLogbook’s private-label platform to provide customers with a seamless, branded UAV management and flight-logging solution. Our customizable system lets you deliver full fleet-management capabilities, ensuring continuous engagement throughout the product lifecycle. This gives your customers a superior experience while providing your team with actionable data and insights into aircraft performance and real-world operations.

    DroneLogbook Mobile Apps

    The DroneLogbook solution is based on a cloud infrastructure with secure data storage, online web application accompanied by our mobile application for offline access and easy synchronization with your cloud based account. In addition, the mobile app provides you with access to the latest airspace status updates, local weather and solar index reading that might affect your operations.


    Another Android mobile application, DLBSync, simplifies import of your flights from major mobile drone flight control apps natively to your DroneLogbook account. This app can sync flights into DLB Sync from your flight control apps when offline or in poor mobile coverage, then upload flights to DroneLogbook account when you have mobile or WIFI coverage.
