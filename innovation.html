Innovation in Drone Operations, Compliance & Data

DroneLogbook is continually evolving to make drone operations, flight logging, and compliance management simpler for commercial, government, and public safety UAS teams. Our platform focuses on three things: removing manual data entry, standardizing workflows across your fleet, and turning flight logs into structured data you can actually use—for audits, analytics, and AI-driven insights. dronelogbook.com

Built on a secure cloud infrastructure, DroneLogbook combines powerful flight-log importers, automation rules, and reporting tools so your organization can manage drones, pilots, and missions from one place instead of juggling spreadsheets and disconnected apps.

Integrations – Connect Your Drone Tech Stack
DroneLogbook is recognized as a leader in flight data import and workflow automation. Our integration engine accepts telemetry files from dozens of hardware and software sources, allowing you to keep flying with your preferred drones and ground-control stations while centralizing your records in a single system. dronelogbook.com

We currently support major drone manufacturers and autopilot platforms such as DJI, Parrot, Wingtra, SenseFly, Autel, Yuneec, Wingcopter, Vantage Robotics, and more, along with leading software tools including UGCS, DroneHarmony, Sky-Drones, Simnet, Zephyr, DroneLink, Flytbase, Q Ground Control, Mission Planner, and others. dronelogbook.com

Once imported, logs are normalized into a consistent format so you can:

  • Auto-populate detailed flight records and pilot hours
  • Visualize GPS tracks and replay missions in 2D/3D
  • Feed data into BI dashboards or AI/ML analysis pipelines
  • Generate regulator-ready and customer-facing reports in a few clicks

Modular & Configurable – Private Label Platform
For enterprises, OEMs, training organizations, and agencies that need deeper control, DroneLogbook offers a Private Label (white-label) platform. This dedicated environment delivers enterprise-grade tools for flight operations, compliance, and oversight under your own branding and URL. dronelogbook.com

Within the private-label admin console you can:

  • Set operational rules and limits for flights and missions
  • Transfer aircraft, batteries, and data between groups or business units
  • Message users and teams and broadcast safety or policy updates
  • Receive notifications when flight or operational parameters are exceeded
  • Monitor compliance, documentation, and maintenance status through a central dashboard dronelogbook.com

As an additional paid option, we can further configure data fields, workflows, forms, and reports so the platform mirrors your safety management system (SMS), quality processes, and internal terminology.

DroneLogbook Mobile Apps

The DroneLogbook solution is based on a cloud infrastructure with secure data storage, online web application accompanied by our mobile application for offline access and easy synchronization with your cloud-based account. In addition, the mobile app provides you with access to the latest airspace status updates, local weather and solar index reading that might affect your operations.


Another Android mobile application, DLBSync, simplifies import of your flights from major mobile drone flight control apps natively to your DroneLogbook account. This app can sync flights into DLB Sync from your flight control apps when offline or in poor mobile coverage, then upload flights to DroneLogbook account when you have mobile or Wi-Fi coverage.
